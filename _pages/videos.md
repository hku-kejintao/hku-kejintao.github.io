[Visit the YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@hkusmlab/featured){: .btn .btn--primary}

Featured Videos
======

Specific YouTube videos can be embedded here once selected. Add an embed block such as:

```html
<iframe
  width="560"
  height="315"
  src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/VIDEO_ID"
  title="YouTube video player"
  frameborder="0"
  allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share"
  allowfullscreen>
</iframe>
```
